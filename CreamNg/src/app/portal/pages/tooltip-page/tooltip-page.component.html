The tooltip directive can be used to display the textual alternative of a svg element. The value of the svg's title element will be displayed in the tooltip.

To apply it, just add the appTooltip directive selector to the direct parent of the svg element.

 
<button appTooltip>
  <svg role="img" width="24" height="24" style="fill: #FFF">
    <title>This is the value of the title element</title>
    <use href="./assets/img/sprite.svg#bt-delete"></use>
  </svg>
</button>
